Diagram Concepts

The central visual idea is the unrolling of a recurrent neural network through time, then tracing the loss gradient backward across that temporal structure.

Forward pass:

x(t1) → h(t1) → y(t1)
          ↓
x(t2) → h(t2) → y(t2)
          ↓
x(t3) → h(t3) → y(t3)


Backward gradient flow:

∂L/∂t3  ←  ∂L/∂t2  ←  ∂L/∂t1
