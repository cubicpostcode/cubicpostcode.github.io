Directive (Plain Language)

I, Cubic Postcode (“Cubic”), hereby declare a total refusal of any medication, ingestible, injectable, topical, inhaled, implanted, or otherwise administered substance that contains atoms outside of C, H, N, O (Carbon, Hydrogen, Nitrogen, Oxygen). I refer to this standard as Pure Organic Chemistry (CHNO).

Standing Order: Do not prescribe, recommend, administer, or dispense any non-CHNO medication or product to me without my explicit, case-by-case written consent.

Rationale

  • Biological coherence: DNA/RNA/proteins/carbohydrates/lipids are overwhelmingly CHNO-based. I choose to align medical chemistry with life’s native code.
  • Metabolic familiarity: Human enzymes broadly process CHNO scaffolds; non-CHNO elements (e.g., halogens, metals) can require special handling pathways, with unknown long-term consequences.
  • Toxicokinetics: Non-CHNO atoms may bioaccumulate and form unpredictable metabolites; CHNO substances more often degrade to CO₂, H₂O, urea, and other familiar metabolites.
  • Ethics & autonomy: This is an informed, conscientiously-held position grounded in personal health philosophy and religious/ethical freedom.

Scope

  • Applies to: Human clinical care, dental, cosmetic, complementary/alternative, and veterinary contexts under my authority (e.g., animals in my care).
  • Allowed: Medications composed entirely of C, H, N, O.
  • Not allowed without written consent: Any preparation containing other elements (e.g., F, Cl, Br, I, Li, Na, K, Mg, Ca, Fe, Cu, Zn, Pt, etc.).
Legal Notice: I may pursue legal remedies against entities or professionals who ignore this directive after being duly informed. This includes, where appropriate under applicable law, regulatory complaints and civil action.

Note: This document expresses my personal standard and religious/ethical conviction. It is not medical advice to others. Clinicians should provide standard of care explanations and risks of refusal; I accept those risks as part of my autonomy.
