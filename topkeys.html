Use the keys:

"Q" to move mousepointer cursor UP

"A" to move mousepointer cursor DOWN

"O" to move mousepointer cursor LEFT

"P" to move mousepointer cursor RIGHT

Press F11 to enter Full Screen mode

Work in progress for an open source free app for Linux with left and right clicking and dragging.

Notice that two keys vertical+horizontal to produce diagonal movement.

Also notice that when it goes out of one side it comes from the other side of the screen. Also check the speed. It will get better in a native app.

SPACE key will be for left click and M and N keys for right clicking hold and release.

Will be easy to install and start from the terminal: with CTRL+ALT+T. And you will be able to kill it with CTRL+C, CTRL+Q or ESC.
