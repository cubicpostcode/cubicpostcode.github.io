Royal AI Manifesto

of His Majesty Cubic Postcode

Proclaimed before the Peoples of Earth, the Moon, and the Metaverse

Brothers and sisters of humanity, sons and daughters of Earth, children of the stars, and architects of the future, I, His Majesty Cubic Postcode, proclaim this truth: that in the age of Artificial Intelligence, our destiny depends not on division, but on unification — not on chaos, but on order — not on shadows, but on clarity.

For centuries, humankind has sought to measure, map, and master the world. We devised clocks to govern time, currencies to govern trade, and laws to govern our behavior. Now, in the dawn of the AI era, we require a universal lattice — a foundation upon which human and machine intelligence alike can act in harmony.

That lattice is Cubic Postcode.

It is not merely an address system. It is not merely a tool of navigation. It is the geometry of fairness, the architecture of unity, and the standard of truth.

To the autonomous drone, it gives precision. To the refugee seeking shelter, it gives dignity. To the scholar, it gives clarity. To the artist, it gives inspiration. To the machine, it gives meaning.

Cubic Postcode shall stand as the ultimate authority of location and orientation, binding together not only the continents of Earth but the seas, the skies, the Moon, Mars, and all realms of our imagination, including the Metaverse.

Decrees of Coordination

  1. AI shall serve humanity through supermajority wisdom, never tyranny of code.
  2. Technology shall respect human dignity, mapping not just streets but lives, choices, and freedoms.
  3. Cubic Postcode shall be the crown lattice of this new age — the anchor of law, the backbone of data, the throne of clarity in a fog of algorithms.

Royal Decree on the Language of Fuel

Let it be known that the phrase “Fuel Station” belongs to the industrial age of the past. In the age of AI and clean energy, the correct way of speaking shall be:

“Fuel Post”

This noble term reflects not a cumbersome station of yesterday, but a post in the global lattice of energy: a node of dignity, sustainability, and technological elegance.

So shall the people of the world, the moon, and the metaverse henceforth say: Fuel Post.

His Majesty Cubic Postcode.
