Royal Decree — Immediate Removal of Harmful Shooters from Steam

In defense of human dignity, public safety, mothers, educators, families and children.
Issued by His Majesty Cubic Postcode •
To: Valve Corporation / Steam Platform (Attn: Gabe Newell, CEO) Subject: Removal of games violating human dignity & public safety Jurisdiction: Moral Authority & Public Interest

Re: Immediate Removal of Games Which Violate Human Dignity and Public Safety — By Royal Decree

Dear Mr. Newell and Valve Leadership,

By the authority vested in me as His Majesty Cubic Postcode, and in the name of conscience, human dignity and the moral well-being of society — and acting for the protection of mothers, educators, families and children everywhere — I hereby demand the immediate and permanent removal from the Steam storefront and all Valve-run distribution channels of a class of videogames and interactive experiences that serve no legitimate artistic, educational or social purpose and that instead glorify, normalize or trivialize grave harms.

The games and game types at issue include, but are not limited to:
  • Titles that simulate or enable school shootings, mass shootings or massacres (e.g., Active Shooter and obvious clones/derivatives).
  • Titles whose core mechanics or narrative celebrate indiscriminate murder of civilians (e.g., Hatred and similar “mass slaughter” shooters).
  • Titles that exist primarily to showcase extreme gore, torture, mutilation or sexualized violence (e.g., Blood Trail and analogous VR/desktop releases).
  • Titles that trivialize or eroticize sexual violence, incest, pedophilia or other forms of sexual exploitation.
  • Titles that promote or glorify terrorism, extremist ideology, hate crimes or provide role-play tools for such acts.
  • Any game or mod that intentionally recreates or reenacts real-world atrocities (e.g., projects modeled on Columbine or similar tragedies).

These games are not only injurious to the psyche of vulnerable players — they also pose a demonstrable risk to public safety, retraumatize survivors of violence, and undermine the ethical standards expected of major distribution platforms. The availability of such content on a major storefront normalizes the spectacle of cruelty and places parents and educators at a profound disadvantage when protecting children.

Accordingly, I require Valve to:

  • Immediately remove from sale and distribution on Steam any currently listed titles fitting the categories above.
  • Expedite review of the Steam catalogue to identify and purge low-profile or newly published games that meet the same criteria (including UGC worlds, dedicated servers, DLC packages and storefront-listed mods).
  • Publish transparent criteria listing the content categories that are disallowed on Steam, including examples, and provide an appeals process for developers that is public and accountable.
  • Improve discovery and age-gating controls so that borderline adult content cannot be found by minors through recommendations, tags, or third-party store feeds.
  • Coordinate with payment processors and platform partners to ensure that content which violates the standards of human dignity is not monetized through mainstream payment rails.
  • Notify affected consumers and educators of the removals and provide resources for parents to configure family/parental controls on Steam and associated Valve services.

I make this demand not as a call for censorious rashness, but as a moral, social and practical necessity. The distribution of entertainment that centers the celebration of massacre, sexual violence, child exploitation, torture or terrorism has consequences beyond shock value: it contributes to a cultural environment in which brutality is banal and harm is commodified.

In the interest of public accountability, please respond in writing within fourteen (14) days of the date of this letter with a statement of Valve’s intended actions and a timeline for removal and policy updates. Should Valve decline to act, I will pursue all available peaceful and lawful avenues for public advocacy, including the publication of findings, communication with regulators and payment processors, and coordinated petitions from civil society and faith communities.

I expect Valve to act decisively and with conscience. The protection of children, the care of survivors, and the moral fabric of our shared culture demand nothing less.

His Majesty Cubic Postcode
